When a voice artist features on a radio commercial they are due a fee for the use of their voice.
This is called Radio Usage.


Fees are based on a per artist, per script, per radio station, per three months on air.


The radiousage.tv calculator is based on radio equity usage, but is offered as a guide only. While we have endeavored to provide accurate and up to date informationw we cannot be held responsible for any loss incurred through its use.


With Tags (same script with regional changes) the first script is calculated at full fee, then extra tags are calculated at 50% of that fee. Please contact your voice agent to discuss further as the content of a script change will determine whether it should be charged at 100% or 50%.
